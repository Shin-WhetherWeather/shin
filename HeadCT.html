Micro-X Head CT Scanner

2023-2024


The mobile stroke unit, developed in collaboration with Monash Design Health Collab and Micro-X, features miniaturised X-ray tubes that enable on-site scanning for both ischemic and hemorrhagic strokes. This functionality allows for quicker diagnosis and treatment within the critical "golden hour" after a stroke, aiming to streamline the process of care in emergency situations.
PCB Design
Surface Modelling
I worked at Monash Design Health Collab with Micro-X to design a mobile stroke unit. The miniaturised X-ray tubes developed by Micro-X enable on-site scanning for both ishaemic and haemorrhagic strokes on site. This capability ensures faster diagnosis and treatment within the critical "golden hour" following the onset of a stroke.

As part of the project, I was primarily responsible for designing the storage wall (shown in blue above), as well as creating visual renders and animations to showcase the design.
Workflow renders were made to visualise the imaging workflow, including a complete workflow animation.
