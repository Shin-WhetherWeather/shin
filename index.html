Earphones

2022


XHN-22 is a pair of earphones designed for fitness enthusiasts. It is a pair of stylish, compact earphones with intuitive controls. XHN-22 uses capacitive sensors to detect finger gestures on the bright red central groove. Swipes correspond to volume adjustments, double swipes skip songs, and taps pause the media. These allow for rapid controls for the athlete on the run while requiring minimal cognitive load.

2023-2024


The mobile stroke unit, developed in collaboration with Monash Design Health Collab and Micro-X, features miniaturised X-ray tubes that enable on-site scanning for both ischemic and hemorrhagic strokes. This functionality allows for quicker diagnosis and treatment within the critical "golden hour" after a stroke, aiming to streamline the process of care in emergency situations.

AI Polaroid Camera v1

2023-2024


The first iteration of the AI Polaroid Camera, created in collaboration with Rowan Page and with photos and videos captured by Maxim Boutin-Reeve, explores the interaction between photography and generative artificial intelligence.

The camera captures a photograph, generates a description of the scene, and then uses this description to produce a new, AI-generated image, which is printed as a physical photo. This process serves as a tool for examining the unpredictable nature of generative AI and its effects on visual representation.
