Whether Weather Clock

2021 - [Ongoing]


The Whether Weather Clock is a project to inspire different feelings and emotions that accompany us throughout different times of the day.
Microcontrollers
Woodworking
The Whether Weather Clock is an attempt to ask the question: what does 'now' feel like? Separating time from numbers into light, colours, sunrise and sunsets, and the weather.
The sky rotates over the day; weather icons show up on the sky as the weather changes. Shadows cast on the wall become the hour and minute hands of a conventional clock.
The Whether Weather Clock is an ongoing project! Check out the project website.
