Task Clock

2022


A project that grew from an attempt at self-care into general care, the task clock is a response to the general stress and anxiety around the organising and keeping track of daily to-do tasks.
Microcontrollers
Woodworking
The forms of the earphones were 'sculpted' through surface modelling. After several iterations of CAD models and resin printed prototypes, the focus soon shifted to animations and renders. These renders were done with Blender.
